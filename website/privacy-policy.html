Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 23, 2025

1. Introduction

R3E Network ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Neo N3 MCP Server ("Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Service. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Information Collection

2.1. Personal Information: When you create an account or use our Service, we may collect certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you, such as:

  • Email address
  • Username or account name
  • Usage data

2.2. Blockchain Information: Due to the nature of blockchain technology, we may process public blockchain addresses and transaction data. This information is inherently public on the blockchain and is not considered private data.

2.3. Usage Data: We may collect information on how the Service is accessed and used, including:

  • Diagnostic data such as logs and error reports
  • Access times and dates
  • Features used
  • API call patterns
  • Other diagnostic data

3. Use of Information

We use the collected information for various purposes including:

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To notify you about changes to our Service
  • To provide customer support
  • To monitor the usage of our Service
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • To improve the functionality and user experience of the Service

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4.1. Wallet Information: We employ industry-standard encryption techniques for any stored wallet information. However, we strongly advise against sharing sensitive wallet information such as private keys or wallet passwords with our Service.

5. Blockchain Data and Public Information

5.1. Public Nature of Blockchain: The Neo N3 blockchain is a public, decentralized network. Any transactions you conduct on the blockchain through our Service will be publicly visible on the blockchain.

5.2. Immutability: Due to the immutable nature of blockchain technology, transactions once recorded cannot be altered or deleted.

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1. Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

6.2. Analytics: We may use third-party Service Providers to monitor and analyze the use of our Service.

7. Third-Party Services

Our Service may contain links to other websites or services that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

10. Data Subject Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access, update, or delete your information
  • The right to rectification (to request correction of your information)
  • The right to object to processing of your information
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent

11. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will also retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information, including personal information, may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those of your jurisdiction. If you are located outside the jurisdiction where R3E Network is established and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the data to our servers and process it there.

13. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@r3e.network.
